<p></p>
<p>As announced by MOE, The Secondary 1 (S1) Posting results will be released
on 19 December 2025, at 9am.</p>
<p>The S1 Posting results can be accessed through the following channels:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>S1 Portal (<a href="http://www.moe.gov.sg/s1-posting" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.moe.gov.sg/s1-posting</a>)
using parentsâ€™ Singpass;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Short Message Service (SMS) via a local mobile number (if provided by
the applicant during the application process); or</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>At the<strong> </strong>student's primary school. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Students do not need to report to their posted secondary schools upon receiving their S1 Posting results.</strong>
</p>
<p>Students posted to <strong><u>Nan Chiau High School</u></strong> should
refer to this <strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/nchs-2026sec1" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">website</a></strong> for
more information, such as the reporting details, booklist, and purchase
of books and uniforms.</p>
<p>Parents of the incoming S1 cohort who are on Parents Gateway (PG) will
also receive instructions from the afternoon of 19 December 2025 for the
link to complete the online registration with the school.</p>
<p>This link will be available <strong>from 9am, 19 Dec 2025</strong>&nbsp;and
will <strong>close at 9am, 22 Dec 2025</strong>.</p>
<p>Parents who do not have Parents Gateway access or miss this registration
period will be required to write in to <a href="mailto:nchs@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>nchs@moe.edu.sg</u></a>, indicating your
child's full name, NRIC, and Primary School.&nbsp;</p>
<p>For appeal cases, your cut-off point must meet our school cut-off for
the posting year, Posting Group 3, and you <strong><u>must offer Chinese Language as a PSLE subject</u></strong>.
Click <a href="https://go.gov.sg/nchs-2026s1-appeal-info" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> for
more information on how to appeal.</p>
