<h2><strong>Vision</strong></h2>
<p>"To nurture a digitally intelligent school community where staff and students
collaboratively embrace emerging technologies, including AI, fostering
a secure and innovative learning environment that prepares future-ready
learners."</p>
<p>培育数智校园共同体，让师生携手拥抱人工智能等新兴科技，营造安全创新的学习环境，培养面向未来的终身学习者。</p>
<h2><strong>Mission</strong>&nbsp;</h2>
<p>"We develop digital competencies and strengthen cyber wellness through
innovative pedagogy, meaningful tech integration, and collaborative learning,
empowering our school community to be ethical users and creators of technology
in an AI-enabled world."</p>
<p>我们通过创新教学法、有意义的科技整合和协作学习，培养数码素养和网络健康意识，使我们的校园社群在人工智能时代成为负责任的科技使用者和创造者。</p>
