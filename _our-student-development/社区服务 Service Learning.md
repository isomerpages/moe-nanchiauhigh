<p><strong>NCHS Service-Learning Framework</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/service_learning_framework1.png">
</div>
<p>NCHS Service-Learning (S-L) programme focuses on inculcating in students
an awareness of issues surrounding them and exercising social responsibility
in their spheres of influence. Through the Service-Learning project cycle
(Ready, Render, Reflect) and using Design Thinking approach, students will
learn to investigate areas of concerns, empathise with others, implement
their plans to create value in the community they live in and play their
part through meaningful contributions to the community.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Overall mission:&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Engage NCzen’s head, heart and hands to develop them into socially responsible
citizens who contribute meaningfully to the community.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enable NCzens to acquire and deepen knowledge, skills, values and attitudes
that empowers them to lead, serve and inspire.</p>
</li>
</ul>
